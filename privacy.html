Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This privacy policy describes how mandibagley.com ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website and related services.

Information We Collect

  • Email addresses — when you voluntarily submit your email through a capture page to receive free resources.
  • Instagram account data — when you interact with automated comment or messaging features, we process your public username and message content to deliver the requested resource.
  • YouTube account data — when you connect your YouTube channel through our dashboard, we access your channel statistics (subscriber count, video count, view counts), YouTube Analytics data (watch time, views), and recent video listings using the YouTube API Services. This data is displayed only to you in your private, password-protected dashboard.
  • Analytics data — anonymous page views, referrers, and device information to understand how our site is used.

How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver requested free resources (meal plans, guides, etc.) via email.
  • To respond to Instagram comments and direct messages with content you requested by commenting a keyword.
  • To improve our website and content based on aggregate analytics.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate:

  • Google/YouTube API Services — to retrieve channel statistics and analytics. Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
  • Meta/Instagram API — to process comments and messages.
  • Resend — to send transactional emails.
  • Upstash — to store email addresses and analytics data.
  • Vercel — to host and serve our website.

Google API Services — Limited Use Disclosure

Our use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically:

  • We only use YouTube data to display your channel analytics in your private dashboard.
  • We do not transfer YouTube data to third parties unless necessary to operate the service.
  • We do not use YouTube data for advertising or profiling.
  • A human only reviews your data if you explicitly request support.

You can revoke our access to your YouTube data at any time by disconnecting YouTube from your dashboard, or by visiting Google Account Permissions.

Data Retention

We retain email addresses and lead data for as long as needed to provide our services. You can request deletion at any time.

Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, email hello@bysamotto.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and we will remove your information within 30 days. You can also use our automated deletion tool.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at hello@bysamotto.com for any requests.

Changes
